- Welcome to Gray Ridge Brewery -




It all began in 1934 in Ridgetown, Ontario.

Lyle and Ina Gray established an egg grading station in the very competitive Southwestern Ontario egg market. Lyle Gray had taken a holiday to Copenhagen, where he tasted his first sip of Tuborg beer. Gray was looking forward to travel home and share this delightful experience with friends and family.


Unfortunately, Tuborg is not accessible in Ontario, as Gray had come to discover following his return.

The craving Gray had for the rich taste of Tuborg was beginning to become unbearable.

He then wrote a letter to Tuborg Brewery with a pitch to collaborate the two wildly successful companies, and wished for the best. Months passed and the letter had been returned with an agreement.


GRAY RIDGE BREWRY was born.
